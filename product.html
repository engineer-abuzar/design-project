Luxury Hand Cream 50 ml

Description:
The Luxury Hand Cream by Janssen Cosmetics is a special edition designed to provide intense hydration and an unparalleled sensation of softness. Its formula, rich in nourishing ingredients such as sugar-based hydration boosters, hyaluronic acids, allantoin, panthenol, and shea butter, delivers deep hydration while leaving a subtle and pleasant fragrance.

Key features:
- Provides deep hydration with short- and long-chain hyaluronic acids for long-lasting moisture, plumping the skin and reducing the appearance of fine lines
- Soothes the skin with allantoin and panthenol, reducing irritation and promoting skin regeneration
- Intensely nourishes with shea butter and sweet almond oil, leaving hands soft and supple
- Absorbs quickly with a lightweight texture that leaves no greasy residue, offering immediate comfort

How to use:
Apply a small amount of cream to clean, dry hands. Gently massage until fully absorbed, paying special attention to cuticles and the areas around the nails. Use as often as needed, especially after handwashing or when dryness occurs.

Skin type:
Suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.

Summary:
Pamper your hands with the luxurious care they deserve with Janssen Cosmetics Luxury Hand Cream, perfect for daily use or as a refined gift.
